Understanding the Graphs

🔵 Blue Demand Curve: Shows buyers' orders (bids) from highest to lowest price. Each step represents one agent's willingness to buy.

🔴 Red Supply Curve: Shows sellers' orders (asks) from lowest to highest price. Each step represents one agent's willingness to sell.

✅ Market Clearing Price (green line): Where supply meets demand. All trades execute at this price.

🟡 Your Orders (yellow): Your bid/ask segments are highlighted to show your position in the market.
