Why Type-3 and Type-1?

We call them Type-3 and Type-1 because Type-3 capital produces 3 times as much emissions as Type-1 capital.

Specifically, the emissions formula is: emissions = k₁ + 3×k₃

where k₁ is Type-1 capital and k₃ is Type-3 capital.

Detailed Rules for the Decentralized Market

Capital Markets (NEW)

Each period, before making your consumption/investment decisions, you will participate in two simultaneous capital markets:

  1. Type-3 Capital Market: Buy or sell Type-3 capital (k₃) with the other 4 agents in your economy.
  2. Type-1 Capital Market: Buy or sell Type-1 capital (k₁) with the other 4 agents in your economy.
How Markets Work:
  • You can submit bid prices (the maximum you are willing to pay to buy) and ask prices (the minimum you will accept to sell).
  • You also specify the quantities you wish to trade.
  • All trades execute at a single market-clearing price where supply meets demand.

How Each Period Works
