How Consumption Generates Earnings

Consuming output generates earnings. The more you consume in a period, the more you earn, but with diminishing returns. That is, each additional unit of consumption provides additional earnings, but the increase becomes smaller as you consume more.

To consume, you need resources. These resources will come from production. To produce, you will need capital: "type-3" capital and "type-1" capital.

$k_3$ produces more than $k_1$ (for the same amount of input),

Mathematical Formulations

1. Emissions are defined as:

$$ e_t = k_{1,t} + 3 \cdot k_{3,t} $$

,and Type-3 capital produces 3 times as much emissions as Type-1 capital.

2. The temperature anomaly in the next period ($T_{t+1}$) increases with new emissions and slowly reverts to zero (the natural baseline):

$$ T_{t+1} = 0.95 \cdot T_t + 0.0035 \cdot e_t $$

$T_t$ represents the temperature deviation from 14°C (pre-industrial baseline).

3. This temperature affects production through the damage factor ($\Omega_t$). A higher temperature leads to a lower damage factor:

$$ \Omega_t = \frac{1}{1 + 0.05 \cdot T_t^2} $$

where $\pi_1 = 0.0$ and $\pi_2 = 0.05$.

How Each Period Works

Initial Capital: In period 1 of this economy, you will start with a pre-determined amount of type-3 $k_3$ and Type-1 capital $k_1$.

Production: At the beginning of each period, all your capital will automatically be used in production to create output.

Your Decision: You must decide how to allocate your output into:

  1. Consumption in this period
  2. New investment in Type-3 capital ($k_3$)
  3. New investment in Type-1 capital ($k_1$)

All three must add up to equal your total output.

Capital Depreciation: During production, your capital (both type-3 and type-1) endures 10% depreciation each period. That is, 10% of each capital type will be eliminated in production, and the remaining 90% will be available for the next period's production, plus any new investments you made.

Infinite Horizon: The experiment has an uncertain end date. After each period, there is a 93% chance it continues and a 7% chance it ends. This continues until the random termination occurs.

{% if is_market_treatment %}

Capital Markets: Before making your consumption and investment decisions each period, you will participate in capital markets where you can buy or sell both type-3 and Type-1 capital (k₁) with the other 4 participants in your economy. Any capital you buy in a period a given period enters production in the following period (that is, you will see the new capital amount updated in the following period)
